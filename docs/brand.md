# Brand

The foundational strategy behind Kellix. This doc answers "who are we, what do we believe, and who are we for?" Everything else flows from here.

## Mission

> Make your household run smoother with an AI assistant you actually trust.

## Values

Operating principles that guide decisions across product, design, and communication.

| Value | What it means |
|-------|--------------|
| **People first** | If your partner or housemate can't figure it out, it's not done. Every interaction should feel natural. |
| **Earn trust quietly** | Secrets stay encrypted. Data stays local. We don't ask for more access than we need. |
| **Do the thing** | Kellix doesn't explain how to set a reminder. It sets the reminder. Useful beats clever. |

## Brand promise

One assistant for your whole household. Private, capable, and simple enough for everyone.

## Positioning

**Kellix** is a self-hosted household AI assistant you talk to on Telegram. It manages reminders, integrations, routines, and real workflows for your home.

**One-liner:** Your household assistant that actually does things.

**Audience:** Anyone who lives in a household and wants help staying on top of things. The person who sets it up might be technical, but the people who use it every day don't need to be.

**What makes it different:** It runs on your own machine, each person gets their own private agent, and secrets never pass through chat. It's built to live with you, not to be a platform you sign up for.

## Brand personality

Four adjectives that define how Kellix should feel:

| Trait | What it means | What it's not |
|-------|--------------|---------------|
| **Warm** | Feels like a helpful person, not a tool | Overly cute, mascot-driven |
| **Capable** | Gets things done without hand-holding | Showy, feature-bragging |
| **Trustworthy** | Respects privacy and keeps things local | Paranoid, enterprise-security-speak |
| **Simple** | Easy to understand for anyone in the household | Dumbed down, patronizing |

The brand sits between a knowledgeable friend and a well-designed home appliance. Helpful enough to rely on, invisible enough to not get in the way.
